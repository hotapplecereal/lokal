Lokal

Lokal is an effort to create a reviewing platform where the focus is on the community and the locals' opinions. Choose a city as your "hometown" and you will only be able to review this town as a "lokal." This makes sure that when you visit a city, the "lokal's" review will only contain reviews left by people who live there or close by.

Founder

Jackson Hogan is an avid traveller and wanted to help fellow travelers experience the abroad from a local's perspective. He is from Provo, Utah, but has spent time in Israel, Greece, France, Italy and England to name a few.
